Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2025. 04. 12

Introduction

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and disclose information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: We do not collect any personally identifiable information such as name, email address, or phone number.
  • Birthdate: We collect your date of birth during onboarding for personalization purposes. This data is not used for identification but helps us improve your experience.
  • Life Event Metadata: We collect metadata related to life events you log within the app. This includes the event name, description, and date.
  • Device Information: We collect a device ID and a generated user ID (UUID) to track user interactions within the app. This helps us personalize and improve the app experience.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To analyze app usage and improve user experience.
  • To provide personalized content and recommendations.
  • To improve the functionality of the app based on user feedback and interactions.

Analytics

We use PostHog, an analytics service, to track user interactions and improve the app experience. The data collected by PostHog is stored on EU servers and is used solely for app analytics purposes. No third parties, except PostHog, have access to your data.

Data Storage

All collected data is stored on your device. PostHog data is transmitted securely via HTTPS to their servers for analysis. We do not have access to your personal data stored on your device.

Data Sharing

We do not share any of your personal data with third parties, except for analytics purposes with PostHog. We do not use your data for marketing or other external purposes.

Your Rights

Under the GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the data we collect about you (however, we do not collect personal identifying information).
  • Request the deletion of your data. However, you cannot delete specific analytics data, as it is stored on your device and handled by PostHog.
  • Object to the processing of your data, though you may experience reduced functionality of the app if you do so.

Children's Privacy

The app is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child, we will take steps to delete such data.

Security

We take reasonable precautions to protect the information we collect. While we use HTTPS for secure communication with PostHog, we cannot guarantee the security of data stored on your device.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and the "Last updated" date will be revised accordingly. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
